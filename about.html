About Us

Incorporated in the year 2013, Aditya Techno Fab Engineering has been moving ahead in constructing an innovative and ergonomic design of Peb Structures and Pre Engineered Buildings which are made out of fine-grade stainless steel and appropriate components. We as a dependable organization are a renowned name in design, construction, fabrication, and erection of Pre Engineered Buildings, which are fabricated from sturdy panel frames, roof, walls, columns and beams, making the building more robust as well as more functional interior design for plumbing, electrical fitting and ventilation. Being a manufacturer and supplier, we aim to address the demands of construction industry by offering them with services pertaining to Pre engineered Building Structures, Prefabricated & Portable Buildings. In comparison to the cost of brick-and-mortar structures, that of our Peb Buildings are one-third lesser, as well as it lasts longer, besides giving possibility of increasing the storage and altering height, width length and dimension. Our business operation also rests in fabricating and delivering pertinent hardware for construction purposes. After analysis of clients' requirement, we are providing most suitable and cost effective solutions to them which meets all the infrastructure related demands.
